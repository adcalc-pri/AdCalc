AdCalc Privacy & data

Privacy & what we collect

Your calculator data never leaves your browser. Every number you type — prices, costs, ad spend, campaigns, saved scenarios — is computed locally in JavaScript and stored only on your own device in localStorage and IndexedDB. It is never transmitted, uploaded, or seen by us or any third party. There is no account, no login, and no server that receives your inputs.

Optional anonymous analytics

We would like to know which pages are used so we can improve the tool. For that we use Google Analytics 4, but only if you explicitly click “Allow analytics” in the consent banner. Specifically:

  • The Google tag (gtag.js) is not loaded at all until you opt in. Before consent, nothing — not even a cookieless ping — is sent to Google.
  • If you opt in, we collect only anonymous pageviews with IP anonymization enabled. No personal data, no advertising or cross-site tracking, and none of your calculator inputs are ever sent (there are no custom analytics events anywhere in the app).
  • Ad storage, ad personalization, and ad user-data signals remain denied at all times (Google Consent Mode v2).
  • If your browser sends a Do-Not-Track or Global Privacy Control signal, analytics stays off and the banner is suppressed.

Your choice is recorded and reversible

When you accept or decline, we store your choice, a timestamp, and the policy version locally so we do not ask again unnecessarily. You can change your mind at any time:

This clears the stored choice and stops analytics on the next page load. You can also clear all locally stored data from your browser settings at any time.

Subscription

If you purchase AdCalc Pro, payment is handled by our payment processor (Lemon Squeezy) under their own privacy policy. AdCalc only stores the resulting licence token locally in your browser to unlock features.

Your rights (EEA/UK GDPR)

Because we do not collect or store personal data on any server, there is no personal dataset to access, rectify, or erase on our side — your data lives only on your device and is under your control. For the optional analytics or any question, the data controller is Artivicolab. Contact: privacy@artivicolab.com.
