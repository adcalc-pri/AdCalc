Privacy & what we collect

Short version: your calculator data never leaves your browser, AdCalc is free with no accounts, and analytics is optional and opt-in only.

Your calculator data never leaves your browser

Every number you type — prices, costs, ad spend, campaigns, saved scenarios — is computed locally in JavaScript and stored only on your own device in localStorage and IndexedDB. It is never transmitted, uploaded, or seen by us or any third party. There is no account, no login, and no server that receives your inputs. Shareable scenario links encode inputs in the URL fragment (#…), which browsers never send to any server — including Google Analytics.

AdCalc is free

There is no paywall, no subscription, no licence and no payment of any kind. Every feature is available to everyone. We never ask for your payment details because there is nothing to pay for.

Optional anonymous analytics

We would like to know which pages are used so we can improve the tool. For that we use Google Analytics 4 — but only if you explicitly click “Allow analytics” in the consent banner. Specifically:

  • Analytics stays denied by default (Google Consent Mode v2). Nothing is measured until you opt in.
  • If you opt in, we collect only anonymous pageviews with IP anonymization. No personal data, no advertising or cross-site tracking, and none of your calculator inputs — there are no custom analytics events anywhere in the app.
  • Ad storage, ad personalization and ad user-data signals remain denied at all times, and ad-identifier redaction is on.
  • If your browser sends a Do-Not-Track or Global Privacy Control signal, analytics stays off and the banner is suppressed.

Your choice is recorded and reversible

When you accept or decline, your choice is stored locally so we don’t ask again unnecessarily. You can change your mind at any time:

This clears the stored choice and stops analytics on the next page load. You can also clear all locally stored data from your browser settings at any time.

Your rights (EEA / UK GDPR)

Because we do not collect or store personal data on any server, there is no personal dataset to access, rectify, or erase on our side — your data lives only on your device and is under your control. For the optional analytics or any question, the data controller is Artivicolab. Contact: email us.
