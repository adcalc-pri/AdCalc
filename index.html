Product mix (blended)

weighted-average unit economics across saved products

What it does: if you sell several products, run the whole calculator on their weighted average instead of one product.

Steps: 1) in the Product panel, enter a product and click Save product (name it); repeat for each SKU. 2) Each saved product appears below — give it a weight (e.g. its share of orders: 70, 30…). 3) Tick Use blended product — every metric now runs on the blended economics. Untick to return to the single product.

How AdCalc calculates

Every figure is forward-modelled in your browser from the inputs. No data leaves the device. Nothing is rounded inside the engine — display rounding only.

CPC            = (CPM/1000) × (1/CTR)   · or direct CPC
Orders/day     = (Ad spend / CPC) × CVR
Gross margin   = Rev/order − COGS − Shipping − Rev×Processing%
Net margin     = Gross − Gross×Refund% − Other fees
CPO / CAC      = Ad spend / Orders/day
Daily profit   = (Net margin − CPO) × Orders/day
ROAS           = Daily revenue / Ad spend
Breakeven ROAS = Rev/order / Net margin
Target ROAS    = Rev/order / (Net margin − Rev×Target%)
New-cust CAC   = Ad spend / (Orders × New customer%)
MER            = (Rev + Other rev) / (Spend + Other spend)
Reported ROAS  = ROAS × Attribution capture%
RPV (all)      = Daily revenue / clicks
12-mo LTV      = Gross margin × Σ retention^k  (k=0..11)
CAC payback    = first month cumulative margin ≥ New-cust CAC
CPM creep      = CPM × (1 + 0.06 × log2(spend ratio))   when scaling up

Scaling scenarios and the profit curve apply the CPM-creep model so larger spend shows realistic auction inflation, not linear extrapolation.
